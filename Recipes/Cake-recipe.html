Cake recipe

Describtion

this cake recipe will make all your family happpy because of its special taste , don't hesitate to try it !

ingredients :

  • 5 eggs
  • 350g flour
  • baking pouder
  • a cup of water
  • 3 cups of sugar
  • a bar of chocolate

steps

  1. add the water and mix with the flour
  2. add eggs and baking poudre and mix
  3. and sugar
  4. pour the mixture in a bowl
  5. bake it for 20min in 300C
  6. whn the cake is ready add the chocilate to make it appears better
